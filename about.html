Technical Principles

Consequence-Weighted Evaluation

AI accuracy isn't enough. I prioritize evaluations based on the business impact of potential errors, ensuring high-stakes tasks receive maximum oversight. I believe evaluation should be narrative-first and continuously verified.

Agentic Utility over Chat

Real value lies in agents that do things, not just chatbots that say things. I focus on building voice-to-action and vision-to-action workflows that solve clear operational bottlenecks.

Platform-Agnostic Architecture

I build for the "Agentic Era" using modular systems (LangGraph, Claude API) that can evolve as models improve, keeping technical debt low and innovation pace high.
