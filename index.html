Product Philosophy

How I Build

Start with the constraint, not the technology.

Field workers can't touch screens. Enterprise teams need ROI proof before adoption. Regulated industries need governance frameworks. The best AI products work backwards from these constraints.

AI should augment, not replace.

My products keep humans in the loop—technicians approve AI suggestions, not the other way around. This builds trust and catches the 7.1% of cases where even 92.9% accuracy fails.

Multi-agent over monolithic.

Complex workflows need specialized agents. Aero uses 5 agents (intake, guidance, QA, reporting, tracking) because a single LLM can't maintain context across all tasks.

Measure what matters to the business.

Accuracy metrics are table stakes. I track hours saved ($2.4M/year for Agentic RAG), revenue enabled ($11M for Translation Platform), and adoption rates (85% Copilot rollout)—the numbers that get budgets approved.
